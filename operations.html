About Us

Air Hoshi was founded on a simple principle: Nothing goes to waste. Headquartered at the remote Kashio Airport in Eiko, we primarily operate retrofitted aircraft that were previously decommissioned by variety of Airlines and Air Force.

We provide the lifeline that other airlines refuse to fly.

Services

  • Pan-Military Logistics: Contracted supply runs for military operations.
  • Remote Area Logistics: Service in regions inaccessible to large airliners.
  • Air-Sea Interlink: Logistics in both the sky and the sea.
