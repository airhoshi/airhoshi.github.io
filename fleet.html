Aircrafts and Fleets

Our fleet consists of refurbished military surplus and rugged civilian cargo liners.

Bell-Boeing V-22 Osprey

The backbone of our emergency cargo division. Enabling logistic in conventionally inaccessible regions. Especially useful during the wet season where road freight is unreliable due to flooding.

Airbus A400M Atlas

Used for transprtation of heavy equipment and vehicle transport. With STOL capability, it can operate at unsealed surface with good flexibility.

Antonov An-72 Coaler

A turboprop legend. Used primarily for connecting remote areas for supply and passenger movement due to it's STOL capability, the undercarrige is modified to operate at unsealed surface.
