Base of Operations: Kashio

Air Hoshi was founded on a simple principle: Nothing goes to waste. Headquartered at the remote Kashio Airfield in Eiko, we specialize in operating retrofitted aircraft that were previously decommissioned by the JSDF (Eiko) and the USAF (Oconian detachments).

Our location places us strategically between the major urban centers and the rugged Oconian islands. We provide the lifeline that commercial luxury airlines refuse to fly.

Services

  • Pan-Military Logistics: Contracted supply runs for remaining base operations.
  • Remote Island Hopping: Passenger service for the residents of Eiko.
  • Air-Sea Interlink: Cargo transfer from Kashio Port to the air.
