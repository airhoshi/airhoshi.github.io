Base of Operations: Kashio

Air Hoshi was founded on a simple principle: Nothing goes to waste. Headquartered at the remote Kashio Airfield in Eiko, we primarily operate retrofitted aircraft that were previously decommissioned by the Eiko Imperial Air Force and the Oconian Air Force.

We provide the lifeline that commercial airlines refuse to fly.

Services

  • Pan-Military Logistics: Contracted supply runs for base operations.
  • Remote Area Logistics: Cargo service in regions inaccessible to large airliners.
  • Air-Sea Interlink: Logistics in both the sky and the sea.
